Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Risn is a local-first app. Your study data lives on your device. We do not operate servers, collect analytics, or sell data.

What Risn Collects

The following data is stored on your device only and never transmitted anywhere:

  • Study sessions (subject, duration, start time, end time)
  • Subjects you create (name, color)
  • App preferences (duration selection, notification settings)
  • Study streak and statistics derived from your sessions

We never collect your name, email, location, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, usage analytics, crash reports, or any information about apps you choose to block.

App Blocking (Screen Time)

Risn uses Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls) to optionally block apps during study sessions. This feature requires explicit permission, only activates when you start a session with blocking enabled, and immediately deactivates when your session ends.

Risn does not log, store, or transmit information about which apps you use or block. All blocking data remains within Apple's Screen Time framework on your device.

Notifications

Session completion notifications are generated locally on your device. No notification servers are involved. We do not use push notifications.

Artificial Intelligence

Risn does not use any AI or machine learning features. No data is sent to AI services.

Third-Party Services

Risn does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or data brokers. There are no third parties with access to your data.

Children's Privacy

Risn does not collect personal information from anyone. Since no data is collected or transmitted, Risn is safe for users of all ages including children under 13.

Data Deletion

To delete all your data, delete the Risn app from your device. All locally stored sessions, subjects, and preferences are permanently removed. There is no server-side data to delete because none exists.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Risn after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
