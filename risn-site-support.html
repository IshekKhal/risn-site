Getting Started

How does Risn work?
Risn is a study timer built around an hourglass mechanic. Instead of counting down, the sand rises as you study — every second you're locked in, the sand fills upward. Complete a session and watch the hourglass flip. It's designed to make studying feel like progress, not a countdown.
How do I start a session?
Select your subject, choose a duration (25m, 45m, 60m, 90m, or Custom), then tap Lock In. The hourglass starts filling immediately.
Can I switch apps or lock my phone during a session?
Yes. Your session continues in the background. The timer is calculated from wall clock time, so even if the app is suspended, the elapsed time is accurate when you return.

App Blocking

How does app blocking work?
App blocking uses Apple's Screen Time framework. Apps you select in Settings → App Blocking will be blocked while a study session is active and unlock the moment your session ends. App blocking requires granting Screen Time permission.
App blocking isn't working. What do I do?
App blocking only works on a physical iPhone — it cannot be tested in the iOS Simulator. Make sure Screen Time permission is enabled in iOS Settings → Screen Time. If it still doesn't work, try toggling the permission off and on in Risn Settings → App Blocking.

Stats & Streaks

Why isn't my streak increasing?
Streaks only increment when you complete a session naturally (the timer reaches 100%). Manually ending a session early does not count toward your streak. Time studied in stopped sessions still counts toward your daily hours.
Does stopped session time count toward my stats?
Yes. Any session you start and end — whether completed or stopped early — counts toward your total hours studied and your subject breakdown. Only the streak requires a completed session.
My session isn't showing in History.
Sessions appear in History only after they end. An active session does not appear until it is completed or stopped. Check the Timer tab to confirm the session is still running.

Data & Privacy

How do I delete my data?
Go to Settings → Clear All History to delete all study sessions. Your subjects will be kept. To delete everything, delete the Risn app from your device.
Does Risn sync across devices?
Risn v1.0 is local-only. Your data stays on your device. iCloud sync is planned for a future update.
